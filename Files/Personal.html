So, who am I?

At my core I am really a soft hearted and playful guy, always trying to help other people in one way or the other. The kind of guy others turn to for support and advice. Partly because I am a good listener, but also because I have a strong sense of empathy, as well as a mind that is great at see things in different perspectives, which really helps me connect with someone and guide them through to new posibilities.

Beside being a big softie, I am also somewhat of an anti-norm fighter. I tend to always question behaviours and the traditional way of doing things, which so often does not make sense at all, to instead find more optimal ways to engage in the world.

With a wide area of interests and a yearning to always learn more and grow in everything I do, I always take on problems and challenges head on. Armed with great intuition and a mind of patterns, learning about new logic-based things has always been easy for me, which also makes me quite flexible and adaptable when it comes to, for example, programming languages.

But what I really love and am passionate about, is creativeness, in what ever shape or form it might take. This is what drove me to become a game developer, to be able to create new experience through the various means that build up games. The art, the gameplay, the world, the content. Games really contains it all, and I love it.
