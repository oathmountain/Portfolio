So, who am I?

For starters, I am a game designer and programmer, soon to collect a bachelors degree in computer science. So now I am about to start my journey into the proffesional world of game making.

And one thing that I really love about game development, is the combination of creativity and logic, which is two big sides of me that I usually find hard to combine.

Growing up I never thought I would be able to work with something as creative as this, and instead relied heavily on only the logical side as a force forward in life to lean on.
But now, having found this, having found the balance, I am never going back!

With a wide area of interests and a yearning to always learn more and grow in everything I do, I always take on problems and challenges head on. Armed with great intuition and a mind of patterns, learning about new logic-based things has always been easy for me, which also makes me quite flexible and adaptable when it comes to, for example, programming languages.
