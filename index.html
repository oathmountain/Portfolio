Well hello there!

My name is Jimmy Edberg, and I have for the past three years been studying Game Design at Stockholm's University.

I am generally a hybrid between the creative and the logical, having a big love and passion for the likes of art, writing, music and dancing, while at the same time having a solid grasp of math, science, engineering and programing.

Within game development my areas of interest vary a lot, and I strive to learn and grow in all these areas. At the present time I have most experience with design and programing, but I have the goal to also improve my graphical skills.

Beside this, I also have a big interest in people and psychology. I am especially interested in communication, relationships, feelings and needs, as well as mental health issues. In my spare time I am an active voluntary in Tilia and Under Kevlaret, two organizations that works towards improving mental health of teenagers and young adults.
